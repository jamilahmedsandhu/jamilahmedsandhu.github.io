Selected Project Achievements

Digital City Riyadh — Serco / Osool RE
Emergency Evacuation — 4 Occupied High-Rise Towers
Challenge

No structured emergency evacuation system across 4 occupied 21-floor towers with up to 6,000 daily occupants across Saudi Arabia's first smart city campus

Action

Designed and implemented a comprehensive Emergency Control System; established the first Emergency Safety Control Centre (ESCC) integrating all 30 buildings under a unified FLS management framework

Result

Zero LTI maintained across full programme; evacuation drills successfully conducted for 6,000 occupants; ESCC system adopted as project standard across all buildings

KAFD Parcel 2 — Saudi Baytur / Parsons PMC
COVID-19 Crisis Management — Live Construction Site
Challenge

COVID-19 pandemic struck during active KAFD construction phase across 7 towers; no established crisis response protocol in place for a 700+ multi-contractor workforce

Action

Developed and implemented full pandemic emergency response plan; established site segregation, quarantine protocols, medical surveillance, and workforce welfare systems; led crisis management across all contractors

Result

Zero fatalities; site operations continued without major outbreak or LTI; pandemic response plan later referenced by Parsons PMC client as project best practice

Saudi Aramco EDRC Package 5 — Arabtec
380 Villas — High-Risk Industrial Interface
Challenge

Large residential construction programme adjacent to active Saudi Aramco industrial facilities requiring full EDRC client approval and sustained world-class HSE compliance throughout

Action

Established full Aramco-aligned HSE management system from mobilisation; implemented firefighting readiness, scaffolding inspection, and workforce training programmes aligned to Saudi Aramco standards

Result

380 villas delivered with Zero LTI; EDRC Aramco approval obtained and sustained across full project lifecycle with zero audit observations raised
